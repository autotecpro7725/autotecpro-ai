STEP 1:

Software Re-Configuration Procedure: 


	a) Please prepare a tiny pin to reset the unit by pressing the Reset Button at the front of the unit. The reset button should be labelled as "RST".

	b) Download the file which is attached to this email onto the computer (e.g., 732-S3.zip, 732-S2.zip, 715-S1.zip). Once downloaded, "extract" the ZIP file. You'll have a new uncompressed folder. Within this new folder, you should see two files; 'zxwfactory.zip' & 'zxwConfiguration_factory.txt.

	c) Copy these two files to the root directory of a USB Thumb-Drive. 

	d) Turn on the engine and wait for the Android-Screen to boot-up. Then insert the USB thumb-drive into one of the USB ports from the Android-Screen.

	e) Make sure you see the USB Symbol on the top-right of the Android-Screen, after you plug in the USB thumb-drive.

	f) A prompt with a message will appear- "Configuration, files detected, install or not?". Either press "OK" or do nothing at all.

	g) The Android-Screen will restart on its own to finalize the 'Re-Configuration Procedure'. After the Android-Screen restarts, if the touch is reversed, hold 5 fingers on the screen for 10 seconds to re-calibrate the touch settings.

	h) Once this process is completed, please unplug the USB thumb-drive from the Android-Screen.

	i) Prepare the USB thumb-drive for the next step, by deleting all the files on it.

Downgrade Procedure: 


	a) Download the update file (e.g., update_e.zip) from the link provided and copy the file to the root directory of a USB Thumb-Drive. (No need to extract the ZIP update file)

	b) Turn on the engine and wait for the Android-Screen to boot-up. Then insert the USB thumb-drive into one of the USB ports from the Android-Screen.

	c) Make sure you see the USB Symbol on the top-right of the Android-Screen, after you plug in the USB thumb-drive.

	d) The 'SystemUpdater' app will open automatically, asking if you would like to proceed with the software update.

	e) Press on "Upgrade" and wait for the Green Bar to load. (This may take more than five minutes to complete)

	f) The Android-Screen will restart on its own to finalize the 'Downgrade Procedure'.

	g) Once this process is completed, please unplug the USB thumb-drive from the Android-Screen.



After the update, you may also need to go to Car Model / Factory setting to change some settings.

STEP 3:

After completing STEP 2, please run a factory reset on the Android-Screen.

Android-Screen 'Factory-Reset' Procedure:

	a) Open the Settings application.

	b) Click on the System tab on the top right-hand-side.

	c) Scroll all the way down and press "Factory Reset"

	d) A prompt will appear- "Restore to factory settings?", please press on "OK".

	e) The Android-Screen will restart on its own to finalize the 'Factory-Reset' Procedure.


After Android-Screen boots back up, if your original reverse camera is not working. Please follow the steps below:

    a) Open the Settings application.
    b) Click on the System tab on the top right-hand-side.

    f) Once you change this option, head to the bottom of the screen and click on Save and reboot.

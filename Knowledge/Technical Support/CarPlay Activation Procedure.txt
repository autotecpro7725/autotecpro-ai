After performing a factory reset, please connect the unit to Wi-Fi. Once connected, CarPlay/Android Auto should activate automatically.

1) Go to Setting – System
2) Scroll down the menu and select “Factory Reset”
3) Go to settings – Systems – Factory Setting (Password: 7890) – Reversing Format – Select “VGA”

However, please ensure the following:

1) Set the correct time zone under Android settings.

2) Confirm the time displayed in the upper-left corner matches your local time.

3) Make sure the internet connection is working properly.

If it’s still not working, try switching to a different mobile hotspot and test again.

Let us know if you need further assistance!

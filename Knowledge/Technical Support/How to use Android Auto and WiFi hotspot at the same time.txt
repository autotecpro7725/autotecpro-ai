How to use Android Auto and WiFi hotspot at the same time

1) Turn on WiFi Hotspot from the cellphone

2) Unpair the bluetooth from the unit and the cellphone

3) Forget the WiFi network from the cellphone with the new tesla screen. 

4) Make sure the WiFi is turned "ON" on the new tesla screen

5) Connect the WiFi of the new tesla screen to the hotspot of the cellphone

6) Use USB cable to connect the Android Auto. 

Now you can use the internet and Android Auto at the same time.

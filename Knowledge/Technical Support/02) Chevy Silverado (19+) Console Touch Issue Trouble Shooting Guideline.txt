Console Screen Touch Issues
If the console screen is unresponsive inside the Console window, first ensure the cable is properly connected, then adjust the Car Model / AC settings:

Steps:

Go to Settings > System > Setting Guide > Car Model/AC (Password: 666888).

Set Protocol to Xinbasi.

Select the correct Car Model. To apply, double-click the model on the left; the system will restart.

If the touch screen still does not respond:

Turn off the car and remove the key.

Lock the car and leave it for 15 minutes.

Restart the car — the touch screen should now function.

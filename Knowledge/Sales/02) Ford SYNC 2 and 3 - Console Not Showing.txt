F150 / F250 / F350 (2015–2021) – Console Not Showing

If the original SYNC interface does not appear in Console mode, please check the following:

1) SYNC Module Connection
Ensure the original SYNC computer (APIM) has been properly transferred and connected to the new system.
2) LVDS Cable
Verify the LVDS cable is securely connected. Try disconnecting and reconnecting it to ensure a stable connection.
3) Car Model / A/C Settings
Go to the Car Model / AC menu and confirm that all settings are correctly selected for your vehicle.
4) Power Reset
Disconnect the power from both the SYNC system and the new unit for about 1 hour. Then reconnect and test again.

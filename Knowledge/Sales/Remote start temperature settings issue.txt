Hi, thank you for reaching out. The reason the remote start feature does not retain previous climate control settings is due to the current system design. While the remote start will successfully turn on the engine and begin warming it up, the climate settings will not be preserved as they were before shutdown. We understand this may be inconvenient, and we want to assure you that a future software update is planned to address this issue and enable the system to retain your preferred settings.

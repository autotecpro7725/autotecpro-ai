How are you doing? For the Toyota Highlander with original navigation and camera system. This system will not be directly compatible with the original camera directly. The system will be compatible with the original JBL sound system and steering wheel control. However, you will still need purchase a separate Hazard light switch separately (Part #: 84010-0E050-C0). We don't carry this part, you can find this part from eBay, Used car part store or Toyota Dealership as well.

The system will not be compatible with the original reverse camera. You will need to manually re-run the wire for the camera or the easier way is to install an aftermarket camera to replace the original one. Also, for the car with Original navigation, you will also need to purchase the hazard light (Part #: 84010-0E050-C0) separately, otherwise, it will not fit to the new system. This part can be purchase from local automotive part store or eBay as well.

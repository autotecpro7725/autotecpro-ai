Dash Camera Specifications
 
4K+1080P Dual Dash Cam with Voice Control GPS & WiFi

Dual Dash Cam in 4K+ and 1080P with WDR and an F1.8 wide aperture and 4K Front Camera (3840x2160P) and 1080P Rear Camera. supports micro SD cards of up to 256GB in class 10 at U3 speed Intelligent Voice Control

Dash cam control is much facilitated by intelligent voice control. Voice commands: Capture picture, show front camera, show rear camera, lock the video, open recording, turn off display, turn off recording.

Wi-Fi and GPS built-in It comes with a GPS Geo-Tagging device that records precise GPS data into video files so you can accurately check Time, Longitude, and Latitude on Google Map by AZDOME GPS PLAYER, and you can remotely control the dashcam using the "AZDOME" APP. You can also download the record files to your phone to edit them and share them on social media platforms.

Wide F/1.8 aperture for night vision enables better photos. lens made of six glass.

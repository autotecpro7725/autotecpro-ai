CarPlay Troubleshooting Procedure

1) Perform a Factory Reset on the unit.

2) Connect the unit to your phone’s WiFi hotspot.

3) Set the correct Time Zone, Date, and Time.

4) Confirm internet is working by opening the Google Play Store (sign-in is optional).

5) Open the Car Auto app and ensure it is activated.

6) Delete all existing Bluetooth connections from your phone. Also ensure the phone is not connected to the original system via USB.

7) Restart your phone.

8) Pair your phone with the unit via Bluetooth.

9) Open Car Auto and allow all CarPlay authorization prompts on your phone.

10) Bluetooth will disconnect automatically — this is normal. Do not reconnect manually.

11) CarPlay and WiFi hotspot cannot be used at the same time.

12) Do not manually change Bluetooth or WiFi settings on either the unit or your phone during connection.

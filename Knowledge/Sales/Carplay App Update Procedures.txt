There have 2 different ways to update the CarPlay /Android software:


________________________________________
Method 1: 

You can connect to the WiFi hotspot from the cellphone and update directly from the software. The video below will show you how to update the CarPlay / Android Auto software. 


Video Tutorial: https://youtu.be/5ZgSPZA5tYs


________________________________________
Method 2:

Step 1: Download the software from the link provided above to your computer.

Step 2: Copy the softwares (*.apk) to a USB

Step 3: Insert the USB Stick to the USB conncetor of the unit

Step 4: Open APK installer or File Explorer (e.g. ES Explorer). Navigate to the folder of the software in the USB,  Cick on it and install the software.
